## Project Overview

**Folaz Supplements** is a fitness-focused brand developed to support gym members and athletes in achieving peak performance, muscle growth, and a healthier lifestyle.

Many individuals struggle with low energy levels, delayed muscle recovery, and uncertainty when selecting safe and effective supplements. Folaz aims to eliminate this confusion by offering scientifically backed, high-quality supplements made with trusted ingredients and transparent product information.

The project includes building a complete brand identity system — from logo and packaging design to a full social media marketing strategy and promotional campaign designed to build credibility, awareness, and customer trust.

---

## Project Objectives

* Establish a strong and competitive fitness supplements brand.
* Provide safe, premium-quality, performance-enhancing products.
* Address the nutritional needs of athletes and gym enthusiasts.
* Increase brand awareness and build long-term customer loyalty.
